Fun Fact!

 

Ever wondered where the term bug came from?The first ever computer bug reported was an actual bug (the insect)!In those days computers filled (large) rooms and the warmth of the internal components attracted moths, flies and other flying creatures. Those creatures then shortened circuits and caused the computer to malfunction.
